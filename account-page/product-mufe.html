How to Use
Reviews (0)

What it is: An intensely pigmented, blendable lip liner that glides on for long-lasting color that won’t transfer, feather or fade.

Benefits: Long-wearing

Ingredient Callouts: Free of parabens, formaldehydes, formaldehyde-releasing agents, phthalates, mineral oil, retinyl palmitate, oxybenzone, coal tar, hydroquinone, sulfates SLS & SLES, triclocarban, triclosan, and contains less than one percent synthetic fragrance. It is also vegan, gluten-free, cruelty-free, and comes in recyclable packaging.

What Else You Need to Know: This ultra-creamy formula glides on with ease and has a comfortable velvet-matte finish that lasts all day. Wear this extreme-performance lip liner alone, or pair it with your favorite lipstick or gloss. It’s available in some of your favorite Artist Color Pencil shades, including Wherever Walnut.

Suggested Usage: -Trace the Cupid’s bow, ensuring the highpoints are balanced. -Define the center of the bottom lip in parallel with the bow of the lip. -Outline the corners of the lips, then trace along the natural lip line to connect all lines. -Sharpen as needed for clean and precise lines. -Pro Tip: To create volume and give the illusion of fuller lips, start tracing your lips from your outer corners to the center of your lips.
